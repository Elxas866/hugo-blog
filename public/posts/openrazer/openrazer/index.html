What is OpenRazer?#

I recently acquired a new Razer mouse (Basilisk V3 X Hyperspeed) and wanted to modify it on Linux as well. That’s when I found OpenRazer. It is a community-led effort to support Razer peripherals on Linux. The driver supports hundreds of Razer devices, if not all of them.

How to use it#

I really love the Linux community 4 things like this. The driver works like a charm. You can install many different programs that integrate with the OpenRazer driver. I personally use Polychromatic to control the DPI, RGB effects and basically every important setting I need.

SecureBoot#

As for many 3rd party drivers on Linux, the drivers won’t be seen as safe by your systems BIOS per default. Luckily, OpenRazer have a tutorial regarding this here: https://github.com/openrazer/openrazer/wiki/Secure-Boot. Just follow the steps and it should work just fine:)
